Cars Unlimited is your ultimate destination for purchasing second-hand cars. At Cars Unlimited, we have a business philosophy that is straightforward – deliver the highest quality vehicles at the most competitive prices in an honest environment. We have a courteous and friendly team of sales professionals dedicated to making your experience at Cars Unlimited a worthwhile and memorable one. Cars Unlimited brings together years of experience in the automotive industry, dating back to the late 1970’s, when Ray Auto Dealer and Premier Car Centre, which specializes in high-end luxury and exotic cars, were set up. Ray Auto Dealer can be considered as the pioneer in the sale of a large range of vehicles at affordable prices, offering the possibility to those on a limited budget to buy a car on hire purchase terms.
